## How to Use

### 1. Enable Free AI
1. Click the ⚙️ settings icon.
2. Get a free key from [aistudio.google.com](https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey).
3. Paste it in.

### 2. Start a Decision
1. **Describe it**: "Should I buy an iPad Air if I have a MacBook?"
2. **Review Options**: AI suggests options -> you confirm/edit.
3. **Chat**: AI asks clarifying questions -> you answer.
4. **Gut Check**: Set your emotional score.
5. **Get Analysis**: Receive a deep, research-based report.
